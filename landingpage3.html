FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DO I OBTAIN ARREST RECORDS SUCH AS CHARGES, COURT DATES, & BAIL INFORMATION?

We specialize in providing inmate information for every county jail within the San Bernardino, Riverside, and Orange counties. Our representatives can quickly get you the information you need. Please provide the defendant’s full name as well as date of birth or age.

HOW DO I LOCATE A PERSON ARRESTED OR CURRENTLY HOUSED AT A COUNTY DETENTION FACILITY?

To locate an inmate who was just arrested or who is being housed at a county jail you will need to fill out the contact form for the specific county on the website. You will need to provide:

  • Defendant's first name
  • Defendant's last name
  • Defendant's date of birth

HOW DO I CHECK TO SEE IF I HAVE AN ACTIVE WARRANT?

If you believe you have an active warrant or are worried about the possibility, it is important that you call us immediately at one of our contact numbers or fill out the contact form in the website. Please provide your full name and date of birth.
